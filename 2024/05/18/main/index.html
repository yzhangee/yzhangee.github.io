About Me

Yongqi will joint DSA department in HKUST(GZ) as a tenue-track assistant professot in July. Before joining HKUST(GZ), he worked as a research scientist in 4Paradigm Inc. and has close cooperation with Prof. Quanming Yao in Tsinghua University. Yongqi received his Ph.D. degree at the Department of Science and Engineering of the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2020, advised by Prof. Lei Chen. Before the Ph.D life, he obtained a bachelor degree at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in 2015.

Research Interst

My research focuses on learning from graph structured data by modeling the relationships among objects, leveraging multiple resources in the world and interpreting the inference processes. Generative models like large language models will inevitably be one of my key research focus in the near future. I am also interested about applying artificial intelligent techniques for interdiscipline research, in particular the biomedical domain. Research on large language models is another important topic in future works.

Keywords: graph learning, automated machine learning, generative AI, AI for biomedicine.

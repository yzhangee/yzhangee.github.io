Research Topics

Our research group primarily revolves around knowledge-integrated Machine Intelligence, with a focus on exploring the integration of domain-specific knowledge with AI techniques. The key areas of exploration include LLM Reasoning, Multi-modal RAG, Agent Memory System, VLA learning, etc.

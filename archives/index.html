Research Topics

My research primarily revolves around versatile learning from relational graph data, with a focus on modeling object relationships, utilizing various resources, and interpreting the inference processes. In the near future, I anticipate generative models, such as large language models, to become a key aspect of my research. Additionally, I am intrigued by the application of graph learning techniques in interdisciplinary research, particularly within the biomedical field.

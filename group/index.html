KiMI: Knowledge-integrated Machine Intelligence

KiMI is a research lab in HKUST(GZ) dedicated to exploring the intersection of artificial intelligence and domain-specific knowledge. Our mission is to develop intelligent solutions that not only learn from data but also leverage existing information to enhance reasoning and understanding.

Our research primarily focuses on seamlessly integrate vast reservoirs of knowledge with cutting-edge machine learning techniques. Key areas of exploration include:

  • Cutting-edge LLMs: We concentrate on improving the efficacy of current large language model (LLM) solutions to enhance their performance and applicability.
  • Industrial LLMs: We focus on applying LLMs to industrial scenarios, leveraging domain-specific knowledge to optimize processes and drive efficiency in various sectors.
  • AI for science: We harness the power of AI to address complex scientific challenges, driving innovation and discovery across various scientific disciplines.

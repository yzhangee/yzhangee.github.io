About Me

Yongqi is a researcher in Research Group@4Paradigm, focusing on automated machine learning and knowledge graph embedding problems. He received his Ph.D. degree at the Department of Science and Engineering of the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2020, advised by Prof. Lei Chen. Before HKUST, he obtained a bachelor degree at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in 2015. He did an internship in 4Paradigm since Oct. 2018 and jointed as a researcher in Mar. 2020.

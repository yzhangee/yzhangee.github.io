Full-time PhD students in Multimodal Reasoning, RAG, LLM & Agents

I am seeking Full-time PhD students in the coming semesters. Students who have strong motivation to start a research career with me and are interested in Multimodal Reasoning, RAG, LLM, and Agents are welcome to contact me.

What I look for

Admission Requirements

University criteria
Meet the university's specified admission requirements — please refer to the postgraduate Handbook for details.
Educational background
Graduates from renowned universities (985 or equivalent) in computer science, electronic information, mathematics, statistics, artificial intelligence, and related fields, or with interdisciplinary backgrounds, are welcome to apply.
Foundational skills
Solid mathematical and algorithmic foundations, together with strong programming skills.
Research character
Strong enthusiasm, excellent self-motivation, and the ability for independent thinking and problem-solving.
Preferred qualifications
Priority will be given to applicants who meet one or more of the following:
  • Prior research or work experience &mdash; Master's applicants should have at least one A-level paper.
  • Outstanding academic performance, with a high GPA or ranking.
  • Active contributions to influential ML-related open-source projects.

Also welcome

RA, Internship & Visiting Students

Short-term collaborations are open year-round for students who want hands-on research experience before applying for graduate study.

  • Research Assistants — on-site RA positions for graduates or senior undergraduates preparing for graduate study with the group.
  • Internships — project-scoped internships for current students who want to contribute to an ongoing paper or system.
